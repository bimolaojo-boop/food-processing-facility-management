# Food Processing Facility Management

A comprehensive food safety platform implementing HACCP monitoring, inspection scheduling, batch tracking, and recall coordination for food processing facilities.

## Food Safety Compliance

Production batches are tracked from creation through release with comprehensive HACCP checkpoint monitoring. Temperature, pH, and moisture levels are recorded at critical control points with automatic compliance validation. Non-compliant batches are flagged immediately to prevent unsafe products from entering the market.

## Quality Assurance

Scheduled facility inspections track compliance scores and document corrective actions. Authorized inspectors can release safe batches or initiate recalls for contaminated products. The system maintains complete traceability for all production activities.

Critical for food processors requiring comprehensive safety monitoring with blockchain-verified quality records and rapid recall capabilities when needed.
